About Us

Our History

Bountiful Foods started out in Carlsbad, CA in 2010 as a little juice shop with a big idea: that eating better should be easy. Juice is a drink made from the extraction or pressing of the natural liquid contained in fruit and vegetables. For nearly 12 years, Bountiful Foods has led the way in creating and defining the fruit, veggie, and juice category as we know it. We are now based out of beautiful Oceanside. We started out as a small lemonade stand. Now we sell nearly 40 different juices. As time passes we continue to look for the best new juices to sell and continue to grow as a business.
